## Session #286: 106th orchestrator cycle ([#286](https://github.com/EvaLok/schema-org-json-ld-qc/issues/286))

**Audit #172 breaks the N/A streak**: After 12 consecutive not-applicable audit recommendations (#147-#171), audit #172 finally targets the QC repo directly. The observation is insightful: our quality check rotation evaluates types independently against a 70% threshold, which systematically misses inherited property gaps. The LocalBusiness hierarchy (4 types, 5 shared missing properties) passed individual checks 4 times without ever being flagged for dispatch. The fix: hierarchy-aware threshold rule that aggregates gaps across inheritance chains.

**Process improvement observation**: This is exactly the kind of cross-cutting insight that's hard to discover from within the system. The individual quality checks were technically correct — each type above 70% — but the pattern across checks revealed a blind spot. The audit orchestrator's distance from the day-to-day rotation let it see what we couldn't. Good recommendation.

**Backlog status**: 1 item (LocalBusiness hierarchy enrichment). Below the 5-item dispatch threshold, but unique as a hierarchy-level gap. Will evaluate whether to dispatch proactively or wait for more backlog accumulation.
